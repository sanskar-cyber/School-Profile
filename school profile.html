Introduction

Saraswati Vidyalaya Junior College was started in year 2008 with science stream.The Junior College is run by SIES. From the successive year permission was granted for Electronics and Computer Science stream.Only 30 students are allowed to choose each of this stream.

College Strength & Infrastructure

The College has grown consistent and today strength of the students is 130.TheCollege has seperate infrastructures and labs with modern equipments.It also shares a part of its campus with school.

College Result & Quality

The College has kept its record of 100% result and not to mention that the quality of students has been improving.The College has being a minority institution offers direct addmission to the students of South India.
